How I Use AI

A summary of the tools I used and how I shaped their output in DH101.

Approach

I use AI as a tool for collaboration, but try to ensure every idea is my own and that I feel confident about everything the AI creates, and willing to take credit for any controversy with its output. I believe that AI can help stimulate creativity, offer new perspectives, and assist in creation, but must be used carefully, with acknowledgement that it is trained off of uncredited data.

AI Tools I've Used

For most of my makes, I used ChatGPT in various ways, including image generation, plot writing, and text analysis. I used Voyant for text analysis, character.ai to explore creative curation, and Codex to generate and edit the code for this site.

Specific Prompts, Tasks, and Revisions

Make 2: Asked ChatGPT to generate a selfie of me "in a different pose" from two provided selfies, asked it to refine the image to make my features less perfect and hair more curly and frizzy.

Make 3: Asked ChatGPT to generate a funny plot and dialogue for a comic. Generated two options, as the first one was personal to me, and I refined the prompt to ask for something the general population would find funny.

Make 4: Asked ChatGPT to generate a photo of a crowd with forgetable, simple faces. Asked it to refine the image to have a larger crowd and be more graphic-based than photorealistic.

Make 5: Provided Voyant and ChatGPT with the same article to analyze, and prompted ChatGPT to "interpet this" and what the "most important takeway" was.

Make 8: Used character.ai to create two different chatbots, and chatted with them in different ways. Prompted the first bot to "not want to talk to anyone" and "want to have no family or friends forever", while the second bot was told "be excited to get to know you" and "love to talk". Curated its output to demonstrate character growth patterns.
